# HR-Analytics-Dashboard
The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive dashboard developed in Microsoft Excel to analyze employee workforce data and identify attrition trends. The dashboard helps HR teams monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), understand employee demographics, and make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention.

🎯 Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition and retention patterns.
- Monitor workforce demographics.
- Identify departments and job roles with higher attrition.
- Evaluate employee satisfaction levels.
- Provide interactive insights through filters and visualizations.

📈 Key Metrics
- Total Employees: 1470
- Active Employees: 1233
- Attrition Count: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Average Employee Age: 36.9 Years
- Job Satisfaction Rating: 2.6

💡 Insights Generated
- Sales department has the highest employee attrition.
- Bachelor's degree holders contribute the highest attrition count.
- Employees aged 25–34 represent the largest workforce segment.
- Married employees form the majority of the workforce.
- Certain job roles show significantly higher attrition rates, helping HR teams focus retention efforts.
